# Purpose of the Free Trial

The **EXL Transaction Insights Free Trial** provides organizations with a simplified, secure way to experience the capabilities of the platform. It is designed to demonstrate how easily transaction data can be transformed into meaningful insights that inform risk management, product strategy, and customer engagement.

Through the trial, you can:
- Upload and process sample or custom transaction data.
- Explore our **interactive dashboards** and visual analytics interface.
- Understand how EXL’s enrichment layer adds context to raw transaction data.
- Review **Customer Profile across multiple dimensions, cash flow summaries**, and **derived risk indicators** and much more .

The trial environment mirrors the production setup — ensuring a realistic experience — while maintaining strict data protection standards. No setup or installation is required; simply register, log in, and start exploring.
