The free trial provides a preview of the analytics, enrichment, and visualization capabilities of EXL Transaction Insights. To unlock the full range of features and integrate insights directly into your business workflows, you can transition to the enterprise deployment version.

- Advanced dashboards with trend analytics and benchmarking
